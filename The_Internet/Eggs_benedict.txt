====== Eggs benedict ======
Created Friday 09 February 2018

egg benedict
https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/eggs_benedict/

8 pc bacon
4 eggs
2tsp rice vinegar
butter

142g unsalted butter
3 egg yolks
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp salt
cayenne

cook bacon
simmer water, add vinegar

hollandaise sauce:
melt butter
mix egg yolks, cayenne in blender
blend on low adding melted butter
add salt and lemon juice
leave in warm place

poach eggs:
simmer water
crack egg in bowl and slip into simmering water (one at a time)
turn off heat, cover the pan, leave for approx 4 min
